[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when our shadows start to sway
You pull me closer and the whole world fades away
There is a magic in the way you look at me
A kind of hunger and a wild, sweet remedy

[Verse 1]
A dusty window and a softly glowing light
We leave the shadows in the middle of the night
You trace a circle on the collar of my coat
And leave a message that you never really wrote

[Pre-Chorus]
The embers burning as I look into your eyes
A quiet fever and a beautiful surprise
I want to wander in the garden of your mind
And leave the worry of the broken world behind

[Chorus]
Caught in the middle of a golden glow
Moving together where the wild winds blow
I want to hold you till the morning breaks
And give you everything my humble spirit takes

[Post-Chorus]
Hold me closer in the fading light
[Echo] (Hold me closer in the fading light)

[Verse 2]
A heavy whisper in the stillness of the room
We breathe the sweetness of the midnight summer bloom
I feel the rhythm of your heartbeat on my chest
A deep devotion where my weary soul can rest
